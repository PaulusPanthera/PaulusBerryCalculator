Current scope

Keep it static, readable, and honest.

Home is the overview. BerryDex is the reference page. Guide gathers the practical knowledge. The calculator tabs stay focused and use shared Prices as the cross-page source of truth.

What is live now

  • Home dashboard with top seed routes, berry routes, Leppa, powder picks, and buy-and-tool breakpoints.
  • BerryDex with sprites, grow time, vendor value, recipe, and deeper seed-harvest breakpoint detail.
  • Seed Routes normalized to a simple standard day with flavor-first comparisons.
  • Berry Routes limited to the intentionally simple families: buy every cycle and pure-color self-sufficient loops.
  • Leppa Planner with the current project-native 30/70 assumptions.
  • Berry Powder routes, Shopping List, Guide, and shared Prices.
  • Harvest Tool fixed at 350 everywhere.

Base structure

  • Framework-free HTML, CSS, and modular JavaScript only.
  • Modules stay purpose-specific instead of hiding everything behind giant abstractions.
  • Shared prices live in local storage and are reused across the calculator tabs.
  • Home and Guide help users find answers faster without replacing the deeper route pages.

Likely next steps

  • Vendor methods / NPC sale page.
  • Shopping List polish like copy/export and add-from-route shortcuts.
  • Later, a more advanced /hour and time-cost layer without replacing the money/day default view.

Small feature first. Honest calculator first.
